# Basic SoccerTwos Tactical Notes

## Defensive rotation
When one player pressures the ball, the second player should maintain recovery positioning rather than overcommit.

## Counterattack pattern
A transition chance is strongest when possession changes in midfield and the far-side player immediately supports forward.

## Common failure pattern
Double-committing near the ball often leaves the goal exposed after a failed challenge.

## Safer shape
If the ball is contested near the wall, one player should challenge while the second stays central enough to protect the goal.
